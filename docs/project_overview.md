# Contribution Reconciliation System (CRS)

## Overview
This is a lightweight SaaS-style internal tool designed to help informal savings collectors (osusu / ajo / contribution managers) track, reconcile, and manage multiple contribution plans across multiple bank accounts.

The system solves a core problem in informal finance management:

> Difficulty tracking and allocating mixed payments across multiple contribution plans when users send combined transfers into a single bank account.

## Primary User
- Contribution collectors (osusu agents)
- Informal savings group managers
- Cooperative finance operators

## Core Problem
Users run multiple contribution plans simultaneously. Participants often belong to multiple plans and sometimes send combined payments in a single transfer.

This creates:
- Difficulty in tracking who paid what
- Confusion in allocating funds to correct plans
- Manual reconciliation across WhatsApp messages and bank alerts
- Errors in payout calculations

## Solution
A simple web-based system that:
- Tracks contribution plans
- Tracks members and their participation in multiple plans
- Records incoming payments
- Automatically allocates payments to correct plans
- Provides reconciliation dashboard per plan and per bank account

## MVP Goal
Replace WhatsApp + notebook tracking with a structured system for:
- Payment tracking
- Plan management
- Reconciliation of mixed payments
